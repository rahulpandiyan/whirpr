Whispr is a simple open-source clone of Omegle, where people get connected to other people, in a one-to-one chat.
If everyone is paired up, you must wait until someone finishes a conversation. Chats are anonymous,
meaning that the strangers you chat with don't know anything about you (unless you describe yourself).
